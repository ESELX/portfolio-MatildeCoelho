Gen 4 remakes are REAL!

Here are some proofs in the latest generation games Pokémon Sword and Shield. Click here to see the trailer PokémonSword&Shield and follow me on my social medias for other Pokémon news and facts everyday! Twitter.

Galar region map

Haven't we seen that somewhere else? That's right, Sinnoh region map is pretty similar to Galar's map. We first start at the bottom of it, in a cozy and countryfield, just like Twinleaf Town. We also see a lake right next to that little town, just like Lake Verity. At the top of the map, we can observe a snowy location, much alike Snowpoint city. Lastly, we can sure compare the massive ammount of mountains in both maps.

Starters

Starting up with Scorbunny, the fire type rabbit, we notice something right up, he has the same exact pose has our little monkey buddy, no, I'm not talking about Grookey, we'll get there, I'm talking about Chimchar, the fire partner from Diamon, Pearl and Platinum games. Grookey and Turtwig share the same color patern, green, brown and yellow (aside from orange) aswell as Sobbles and Piplup, blue, white and yellow.

Trainers

At first look they seem completly different don't they? Well, let's see from an uneven perspective, firstly, they all come from a cold weather region, making them wear beanies and long coats. Now if you look closely, you can notice how their eyes and eyebrows are also identic in terms of shape. Last but not least, going back to their clothing, as we know Lucas, the male trainer from Pokémon Diamond, Pearl and Platinum version, wears a red shirt and where do we see that? That's right, in the male protagonist from Sword and Shield. Same happens with Dawn, as she wears a pink dress and so does the female protagonist from the newest games.
